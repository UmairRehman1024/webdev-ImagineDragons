Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, consisting of lead singer Dan Reynolds, guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee and drummer Daniel Platzman.

The band was formed in 2008. They made 3 EPs Imagine Dragons EP, Hell and Silence EP and It's Time. They released their debut studio album Night Visions on September 4, 2012. And 3 more Albums after that.

The band was named Amazon.com's "Favorite Rock artist of 2012" and was listed as one of "2012's Brightest New Stars" by Billboard Magazine.
